WorldSkills Competition - Kazan 2019


After a year of preparation for the WorldSkills competition Kazan 2019, which was attended by 1300 competitors from all over the world, the effort really paid off. Congratulations to Josip, but also to his/ours mentors who prepared him for this competition - to the head of systems engineering studies, Silvio To Papić, head of the Department of Operating Systems - Vedran Dakić and an assistant in the study of systems of Engineering – Jasmin Redžepagić. Congratulations to ours too to a 3rd-year undergraduate program student of engineering to Josip Horvat and mentor to the head of the department for software engineering - Goran Đambic for winning 16th place in the skill IT Software Solutions for Business.
